What is SDG-4?

SDG-4 aims to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all by 2030. It emphasizes removing inequalities, improving learning resources, and supporting both children and adults worldwide.

Key targets of SDG-4 include universal primary and secondary education, affordable technical training, and the elimination of discrimination in learning environments.
